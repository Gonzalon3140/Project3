Why use Thyme Is Money?



According to Harvard School of Public Health, there are numerous benefits associated with weekly meal planning. In fact, planning your meals can be a "helpful healthy eating strategy". In this crazy work-a-day world, we often think of food as something we "have to" take care of and quickly grab fast food rather than sitting down to a balanced meal made with fresh ingredients --- meal planning to end this cycle. Harvard notes there are other important benefits of meal prep:

  • Can help save money
  • Can ultimately save time
  • Can help with weight control, as you decide the ingredients and portions served
  • Can contribute to an overall more nutritionally balanced diet
  • Can reduce stress as you avoid last minute decisions about what to eat, or rushed preparation
